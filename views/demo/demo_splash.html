Goals

  • Minimize our customers' development expense by providing off-the-shelf software.
  • Save time for web site owners by preventing invalid complaints and streamlining business processes.
  • Foster healthy communications with copyright owners by making a good user experience.
  • Make our customers hard targets for lawsuits.
  • Raise the bar on a component which is ubiquitous across the web.

Features

  • Replaces static text legal boilerplate with hosted app.
  • Saves web sites time on data entry for each request by getting copyright owners to fill out a form.
  • Prevents invalid complaints by improving usability and educating users.
  • Prevents frivolous complaints by setting a serious tone in design.
  • Makes it easier to figure out what the notification is in regard to.
  • Reduces copyright owner work by supporting multiple submissions per notification.
  • Relaxes copyright owners by providing easily readable type, attractive design, and good user experience.
  • Saves web sites time by providing FAQs to point to or copy.
  • Reduces likelihood of a legal mistake by catching small details required by law.
