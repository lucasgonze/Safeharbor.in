Restart Demo
 

You just saw our web app for submitting copyright complaints to a web site. It improves on prior art with features in support of these customer goals:

  • Minimize custom development

    • Our hosted app is the only off-the-shelf package on the market.

    • It includes a form for editing DMCA requests, help for users, branding, and a look which conveys quality.

  • Prevent invalid complaints and streamline business processes.

    • Copyright owners, rather than web sites, do the data entry for each request.

    • When copyright owners do use email instead of the form, help them get it right.

    • Help text and inline help reduce complaints that aren't about copyright.

    • There are pre-written answers to standard questions.

    • Posters who don't have legal standing are discouraged.

    • Frivolous complaints are discouraged by using design to convey seriousness.

  • Be a hard target for lawsuits.

    • Get the details right.

    • The look and feel of quality software says that the web site takes the law seriously.

  • Avoid conflict with copyright owners by using their time efficiently.

    • Allow multiple notifications per submission, which means less work and fewer mistakes.

    • Readable typography makes it easier to fill in the form correctly.
