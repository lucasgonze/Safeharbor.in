Before submitting this form

This form only relates to alleged copyright infringement. If you wish to report something else, this is not the place to do it.

Only the actual copyright owner or their legally authorized agent can report copyright infringement. If you are neither the owner nor the registered agent of a copyright owner, please tell the person who is, and direct them to this page.

Bypassing this form

Submitting this online form is the most reliable and efficient way to communicate your concern to us. If you prefer to submit notification directly, you may use postal mail or send an email.
