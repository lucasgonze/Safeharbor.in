Safeharbor handles DMCA takedown notices for search engines, user generated content sites and ISPs

Our Product

We have created reusable forms for accepting DMCA takedown requests. Our forms are exhaustively double checked for problems and optimized for efficient processing on receipt.

Our Solution

We know that this is not just a software problem. We are here to help with buying and configuring third-party software, forms for submitting requests, workflow processes and training.

Our Services

We have devoted our time and energy to figuring out how to solve the problems you have. Whatever issues you are presently dealing with, come talk to us about maintenance and improvements.

Get it right

It's easy to make a mistake with DMCA rules, and sites often do. Because we're specialists we're less likely to make a mistake, and because our software is shared across many sites we can invest more time in the details.

Worry Less

Copyright lawsuits are a huge risk. A trivial mistake can easily balloon into millions of dollars in lawsuits. Don't just cross your fingers and hope - outsourcing to us means we take on some of the worrying.

Save Money

Doing it yourself is the expensive way to do it, since so much DMCA infrastructure is the same from one company to another.

Stay Focused

You need to concentrate on what you do that's unique, but that doesn't mean you can afford to leave yourself exposed. Using us to deal with the DMCA means your time and attention can be devoted to your unique value proposition.

Our Mission

We specialize in technology for handling DMCA requests because raising the bar on this technology can create a more fair world for web sites, for their users, and for copyright owners.

On the internet, authors have a hard time enforcing their copyrights. People who upload content to sites like YouTube get pushed around by copyright owners. Web sites have too much risk and too big a burden. We think that really well made technology for implementing the DMCA can reduce all these harms.

"Companies often do a suboptimal job because legal risk is not core to their mission. So we made it our core mission." -Lucas Gonze

Frequently Asked Questions how Safeharbor helps with DMCA compliance

How does the solution work?

We provide a suite of products and services around your workflow for handling DMCA requests. That includes programming, training, and third party customer support software.

Why it's so important to get it right

For a web site, a copyright lawsuit is a nightmare. At best it's expensive and distracting, at worst the company goes out of business. You're better off preventing lawsuits than defending them.
