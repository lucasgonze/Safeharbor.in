Get it right

It's easy to make a mistake with DMCA rules, and sites often do. Because we're specialists we're less likely to make a mistake, and because our software is shared across many sites we can invest more time in the details.

Worry Less

Copyright lawsuits are a huge risk. A trivial mistake can easily balloon into millions of dollars in lawsuits. Don't just cross your fingers and hope - outsourcing to us means we take on some of the worrying.

Save Money

Doing it yourself is the expensive way to do it, since so much DMCA infrastructure is the same from one company to another.

Stay Focused

You need to concentrate on what you do that's unique, but that doesn't mean you can afford to leave yourself exposed. Using us to deal with DMCA means your time and attention can be devoted to your unique value proposition.

Our Mission

We specialize in technology for handling DMCA requests, because raising the bar on this technology can create a more fair world for web sites, for their users, and for copyright owners.

Companies often do a suboptimal job because legal risk is not core to their mission. But for us it is precisely our mission, and so we do the best job we can.
