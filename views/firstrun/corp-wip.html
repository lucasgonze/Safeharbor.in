Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?

We provide a suite of products and services around your workflow for handling DMCA requests. That includes programming, training, and third party customer support software.

Why it's so important to get it right

For a web site, a copyright lawsuit is a nightmare. At best it's expensive and distracting, at worst the company goes out of business. You're better off preventing lawsuits than defending them.

Our Mission

We specialize in technology for handling DMCA requests because raising the bar on this technology can create a more fair world for web sites, for their users, and for copyright owners.

On the internet, authors have a hard time enforcing their copyrights. People who upload content to sites like YouTube get pushed around by copyright owners. Web sites have too much risk and too big a burden. We think that really well made technology for implementing the DMCA can reduce all these harms.

"Companies often do a suboptimal job because legal risk is not core to their mission. So we made it our core mission." -Lucas Gonze
